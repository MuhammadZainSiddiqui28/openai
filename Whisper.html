Introduction

Whisper is a general-purpose speech recognition model. It is trained on a large dataset of diverse audio and is also a multi-task model that can perform multilingual speech recognition as well as speech translation and language identification.

Whisper Community

Whisper is a community-driven platform that celebrates authenticity and empathy. Users from all walks of life come together to engage in open and honest dialogue, forging connections based on shared experiences and emotions.

Sharing Moments

Whether you're seeking support during difficult times, sharing moments of triumph, or simply looking to connect with like-minded individuals, Whisper provides a safe and welcoming environment to express yourself freely.

Explore the Depths

Whisper encourages users to explore the depths of their thoughts and emotions, fostering a sense of belonging and understanding in an increasingly fragmented world. Join the millions who have found solace and camaraderie on Whisper.
